Pricing

Finding the right photo package for you is crucial! As of now, I offer Senior Portrait, Family Portrait, Wedding, and Individual photo package options. Upon selecting a package, reach out to me through my contact page, where we will be in touch for session details and to answer any questions you may have!

Senior Portraits

$ 200

A 90-minute session, included with 2 locations and 3 outfit changes. This package includes 40 edited photos chosen by Aliya. Outfit selection and location advice, as well as suggested poses will be given before session to ensure clients look amazing!

Family Portraits

$ 100

A 45-minute session, included with 2 locations. This package will include 30 edited photos of Aliya's choosing.

Weddings

$ 400

Up to 3 hours of total shooting time. Coverage on both bride and groom getting ready, special details, wedding ceremony, both bridal parties, and the reception. The package includes a total of 200 edited photos. Pose and background suggestions will be made, but requests are happily accepted!

Individuals

$ 150

A 60-minute session, included with up to 2 locations, and up to 3 outfit changes. Props and fun clothing is encouraged!
